Gym Climb Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Gym Climb is a simple climbing log for iPhone and Apple Watch. The app is designed to keep your climbing data private and under your control.

Data Gym Climb Stores

Gym Climb stores the climbs you enter, including details such as grade, climbing style, route color, result, effort, notes, and timestamps.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your climbing log is stored on your device. When iCloud is available, your data may sync through your private Apple iCloud account so it can stay available across your own devices. The developer does not operate a separate account system or server for your climbing log.

Tracking and Advertising

Gym Climb does not track you across apps or websites. Gym Climb does not include third-party advertising or third-party analytics SDKs.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell your data and does not share your climbing log with third parties. Data synced through iCloud is handled by Apple under your Apple account settings.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete climbs inside the app where deletion controls are provided. You can also remove app data from your device or iCloud through Apple system settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer at xaq@onthedrums.net.
