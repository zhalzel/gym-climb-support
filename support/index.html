Gym Climb Support

Gym Climb is a lightweight climbing log for iPhone and Apple Watch.

Using the App

No account is required. Open the app, log each climb, and review your current session or history. If iCloud is available, your climbs may sync through your private Apple iCloud account across your own devices.

Getting Help

For support, bug reports, or feature requests, email the developer at xaq@onthedrums.net.

What To Include

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • Whether you are also using the Apple Watch app.
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.

Privacy

Read the Gym Climb Privacy Policy.

Safety

Gym Climb is a personal logbook. It does not provide medical, coaching, or climbing safety advice. Use your own judgment, follow gym rules, and consult qualified professionals when needed.
